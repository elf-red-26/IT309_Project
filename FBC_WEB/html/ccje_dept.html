College of Teacher Education, Arts and Sciences (CTEAS)

College of Criminal Justice Education (CCJE) is committed to developing skilled, disciplined, and ethical professionals in the field of law enforcement, public safety, and criminology. Through a balanced curriculum of theoretical knowledge, practical training, and field experience, **CCJE** equips students with the competencies needed for careers in policing, forensic science, corrections, and other areas of the criminal justice system.

Bachelor of Science in Criminology (BSCrim)

The Bachelor of Science in Criminology (BSCrim) program prepares students to become competent professionals in the field of criminology and criminal justice. The program provides students with a strong foundation in criminological theories, criminal law, law enforcement, and forensic science. It equips students with the necessary knowledge and skills to analyze crime patterns, investigate criminal cases, and contribute to crime prevention and law enforcement efforts.
