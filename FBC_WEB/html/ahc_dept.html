Allied Health Colleges (AHC)

Allied Health Colleges (AHC) is dedicated to preparing future healthcare professionals with the knowledge, skills, and compassion needed to excel in the medical field. Through rigorous academic training, hands-on clinical experiences, and a commitment to ethical healthcare practices, AHC equips students to provide quality patient care and contribute to community well-being.

Courses we offer...

Bachelor of Science in Pharmacy (BSP)

The Bachelor of Science in Pharmacy (BSP) program is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in pharmaceutical sciences, pharmacy practice, and patient care. It prepares students to become competent pharmacists who can dispense medications, counsel patients on the proper use of medications, and provide drug information to healthcare professionals.

Bachelor of Science in Radiologic Technology (BSRT)

The Bachelor of Science in Radiologic Technology (BSRT) program is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in radiologic sciences, patient care, and medical imaging. It prepares students to become competent radiologic technologists who can perform diagnostic imaging procedures such as X-rays, computed tomography (CT) scans, and magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) scans.

Bachelor of Science in Medical Technology (BSMT)

The Bachelor of Science in Medical Technology (BSMT) program is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in medical laboratory sciences, laboratory medicine, and patient care. It prepares students to become competent medical technologists who can perform laboratory tests to help diagnose, treat, and monitor diseases.

Bachelor of Science in Midwifery (BSMid)

The Bachelor of Science in Midwifery (BSMid) program is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in midwifery, maternal and child health, and patient care. It prepares students to become competent midwives who can provide comprehensive care to women during pregnancy, childbirth, and the postpartum period. Students will gain skills in prenatal care, labor and delivery support, newborn care, and reproductive health education.
