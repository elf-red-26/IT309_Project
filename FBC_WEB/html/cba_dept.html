College of Business and Accountancy (CBA)

College of Business and Accountancy (CBA) aims to prepare future business leaders, entrepreneurs, and financial professionals with the necessary knowledge, skills, and ethical values for success in the corporate world. The program combines academic excellence with practical experience, preparing students for careers in business management, marketing, accounting, finance, and entrepreneurship.

Courses we offer...

Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management (BSHM)

The Bachelor of Science in Hospitality Management (BSHM) program prepares students to become qualified professionals in the hospitality industry. The program provides students with a solid foundation in the principles and practices of hospitality management. It equips students with the necessary knowledge and skills to manage hotels, restaurants, and other hospitality establishments.

Bachelor of Science in Accounting (BSA)

The Bachelor of Science in Accounting (BSA) program prepares students to become competent accountants who can work in various industries. The program provides students with a strong foundation in accounting principles, theories, and practices. It equips students with the necessary knowledge and skills to perform accounting functions such as auditing, tax preparation, and financial reporting.

Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (BSBA)

The Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (BSBA) program prepares students to become competent business professionals who can work in various industries. The program provides students with a strong foundation in business principles, theories, and practices. It equips students with the necessary knowledge and skills to manage business operations, analyze financial data, and develop business strategies.
