College of Teacher Education, Arts and Sciences (CTEAS)

College of Teacher Education, Arts, and Sciences (CTEAS) aims to develop future educators, scholars, and professionals with a strong academic foundation, innovation, and ethical leadership. Through modern teaching methodologies, hands-on training, and interdisciplinary learning, the college prepares graduates for impactful careers in education, arts, and sciences.

Courses we offer...

Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEED)

The Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEED) program prepares students to become competent elementary school teachers who can teach various subjects to young learners. The program provides students with a strong foundation in education principles, theories, and practices. It equips students with the necessary knowledge and skills to develop lesson plans, manage classrooms, and assess student learning.

Bachelor of Secondary Education (BSED)

The Bachelor of Secondary Education (BSED) program prepares students to become competent high school teachers who can teach specialized subjects to adolescent learners. The program provides students with a strong foundation in education principles, theories, and practices. It equips students with the necessary knowledge and skills to develop curriculum, manage classrooms, and assess student learning.
