College of Engineering and Computer Studies (CECS)

College of Engineering and Computer Studies (CECS) focuses on developing skilled professionals in engineering and information technology. The program offers a dynamic curriculum that integrates theoretical learning with hands-on experience through laboratories, industry partnerships, and real-world projects. Faculty members are industry experts, guiding students in problem-solving, research, and technical excellence.

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT)

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) program prepares students to be IT professionals who are able to perform installation, operation, development, maintenance, and administration of computer applications. The goal of the program is to gear up students as “information technology professionals”, who can assist organizations in the implementation and management of computer technologies. It also aims to provide students with the necessary knowledge and skills to take on appropriate IT positions upon graduation.

Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (BSCpE)

The Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (BSCpE) program is a combination of electrical engineering and computer science. Its curriculum provides students with a foundation in basic science, mathematics, software, and engineering. The program prepares students to design, develop, and test computer systems and their components. It also equips students with the skills to analyze and design digital systems and computer networks.

Bachelor of Science in Electronics & Communications Engineering (BSECE)

The Bachelor of Science in Electronics and Communications Engineering (BSECE) program is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in mathematics, natural sciences, and engineering sciences. It prepares students to become competent electronics and communications engineers who can design, install, operate, and maintain electronic and communication systems.

Bachelor of Science in Civi Engineering (BSCE)

The Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering (BSCE) program is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in mathematics, natural sciences, and engineering sciences. It prepares students to become competent civil engineers who can design, construct, and maintain infrastructure projects such as buildings, roads, bridges, and water supply systems.

Bachelor of Library and Information Science (BLIS)

The Bachelor of Library and Information Science (BLIS) program is designed to provide students with a strong foundation in library and information science. It prepares students to become competent librarians and information professionals who can organize, manage, and disseminate information in various formats.
